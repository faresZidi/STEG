Signing of the New Agreement between STEG and Banque Zitouna

On August 7, 2024 at the STEG headquarters, under the auspices of the Minister of Industry, Mines, and Energy and the National Agency for Energy Management (ANME), the Tunisian Electricity and Gas Company "STEG" and Banque Zitouna signed a financing agreement for the national program PROSOL Thermal 2024-2026 for an amount of 98 million dinars.
