Natural Gas Program

STEG aims to strengthen the penetration of natural gas in areas already served and to extend its distribution network to new unserved areas in order to benefit all social categories while reducing state expenses on LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) subsidies. As part of this program to encourage the use of natural gas, STEG targets one million subscribers by 2020, connecting 70,000 customers per year.
