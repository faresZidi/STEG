The Documentation Center

The Scientific and Cultural Center has recently been renovated to align with STEG's policy of openness to the knowledge society and its desire to anchor and enhance cultural activity within the company.

The doors of this modern and welcoming space are open to the company's internal public as well as to researchers, academics, and students seeking specialized information.

The Components of the Center

  • Reading Area
  • Multimedia Area

Library Services

Open to the public, the library offers a variety of services, giving users the opportunity to consult the documentary collection remotely via the intranet site. Titles can be reserved remotely for company users and on-site for external visitors.

Library Collection

  • Technical and scientific works
  • Reference works: dictionaries and linguistic encyclopedias
  • Technical and cultural journals
  • Tunisian and international standards (NT / AFNOR / ISO / IEC)
  • National and international newspapers
  • Publications from the official printing office
