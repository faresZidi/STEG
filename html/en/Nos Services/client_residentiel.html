Connection to the Electricity and/or Gas Network

Subscription to a New Service

To connect your residence to the STEG electricity and/or gas network and benefit from electricity and gas services, please visit your district office with the following necessary documents:

For Owners:

  • A copy of the National Identity Card.
  • Original property certificate issued recently by the Property Conservation Office (3 months),
    Or a certified copy of the Sales Contract,
    Or an Occupancy Certificate issued by the competent authorities.
  • Building Permit, Otherwise Municipal or Regional Electrification Authorization.
  • Plan extract attached to the building permit.
  • A copy of the business license and commercial registry for any commercial use.

For Tenants: Premises already connected to electricity

  • A copy of the National Identity Card.
  • A certified copy of the Rental Agreement registered with the tax office.
  • Legalized authorization from the owner (to prevent claims in case of disputes, fraud, or litigation).
  • A copy of the business license and commercial registry for commercial use.

To request a new gas connection for a premises already connected to electricity, please provide a STEG consumption invoice for the premises in question.

Our reception agent will:

  • Advise you on the power capacity of the meter to be installed.
  • Provide the dimensions of niches (electricity and gas).
  • Inform you about payment procedures.
  • Schedule an appointment for the study.

The work will be executed after the payment of a connection quote and a refundable advance on consumption upon the termination of your subscription.
