Daily Actions to Preserve Energy for Future Generations
Eco-Responsible Behavior

Traditional energy sources are becoming scarce, pollute the environment, and are increasingly expensive. Therefore, energy conservation and management have become essential for sustainable development due to their close relationship with economic, social, and environmental progress for current and future generations.

The implementation of good practices among consumers contributes to the national effort to manage energy. By adopting responsible behavior towards future generations (eco-responsible), we can ensure the effective management of our energy resources and their rational use.

We suggest simple actions that can reduce your energy bill and protect your budget without compromising your comfort.
