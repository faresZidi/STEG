STEG Participations

STEG International Services (SIS)

STEG International Services (STEG-IS) is a public limited company with a share capital of 50 million TND, of which 35% is owned by STEG. This company, established through a public-private partnership, was created to promote STEG's experience and expertise in areas such as planning, study, implementation, maintenance, and operation of electricity and gas facilities.

For more information, visit the website: www.steg-is.com

STEG Renewable Energies (SER)

STEG Renewable Energies (STEG-ER) is a public limited company with a share capital of 1.25 million TND, of which 35% is owned by STEG. Established in 2010 as part of a public-private partnership, STEG-ER aims to promote renewable energies and contribute to the Tunisian Solar Plan.

For more information, visit the website: www.steg-er.com.tn
