The Tunisian Electricity and Gas Company (STEG), established under legal statutes, has the following core missions:

  • Electrification of the country
  • Development of the Natural Gas network
  • Construction of electricity and gas infrastructure

Our Expertise

STEG is responsible for producing electricity and Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) as well as for transporting and distributing electricity and natural gas.

Our Vision

STEG is committed to a performance-driven strategy. It aims to provide its clients with services of a quality comparable to those offered by the best electricity and gas companies in the Mediterranean region.
