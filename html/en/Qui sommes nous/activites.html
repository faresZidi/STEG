STEG ensures the production of electrical energy and LPG, as well as the transportation and distribution of electricity and natural gas nationwide.

Its main objective is to supply the national market with electricity and gas energy and meet the needs of all its clients (residential, industrial, tertiary, etc.).

  • Electricity production from various sources (thermal, hydro, wind, ...)
  • Electricity transportation: Management and development of High Voltage networks and stations.
  • Electricity distribution: Management and development of Medium and Low Voltage networks and stations.
  • Natural gas development and distribution: Management of gas infrastructure.
  • LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) production
